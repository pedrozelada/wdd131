YouTube Channel Reviews

Lately, there has been a wealth of information and tutorials available on the internet, with YouTube serving as a notable platform hosting various educators, students, and individuals who create videos to explain mathematical concepts. One notable advantage of learning from these videos is the flexibility to learn at your own pace. If there's a concept not well understood, students can rewatch the videos and pay closer attention. The objective of this website is to showcase these channels, provide brief histories, highlight the quality of the content, and offer reviews.

Dot Physics. This channel explains how to solve physics exercises step by step in a clear way, there are also some Python tutorials, it has several playlists where it teaches mechanics, waves, electromagnetism and many other things.

Minutephysics. This channel is very useful for understanding concepts. In its videos it generally uses handmade designs to explain complex things in an easy way. It is very useful for understanding theoretical concepts and then studying the practical part.

PBS Space Time. This channel is more focused on astrophysics, here you will find explanations of phenomena in the universe, black holes, interstellar travel and many other things, the author explains all these things in simple terms.

Matematicas con Juan. This channel is more focused on mathematics, but it contains several videos where it teaches practical exercises in mechanics, thermodynamics, and electromagnetism from scratch. This channel teaches in a fun way
